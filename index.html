How can Tagengo help you?

Pointing the device's camera at real-world objects to display their names and pronunciations in the target language.

Virtual Tours

Offering AR-based virtual tours of famous landmarks, museums, or cultural sites, accompanied by language learning content related to the location.

Speech Recognition

Real-time feedback on pronunciation using AR avatars that respond to user input.

AR Quests and Challenges

Engaging users with AR-based quests or challenges that require language skills to complete.

Multi-User Sessions

Allowing multiple users to interact in a shared space for collaborative language learning activities and games.

Adaptive Learning Paths

Customizing the learning experience based on user progress and preferences highlighting areas needing improvement.

How it Works!

Tagengo aims at overlaying translations, pronunciation guides, and cultural context onto real-world objects using AR and AI technology, helping learners associate words in their live environment. Learners can interact with virtual objects and characters, making the learning process more engaging and memorable.
